Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

I require at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice will be charged the full session fee.

What materials do students need to have?

Students should come prepared with their textbook, recent homework assignments, any relevant notes, and a calculator if applicable. For online sessions, a reliable internet connection and a device with a camera are required.

Do you offer in-person or online tutoring?

Both! I offer online tutoring for students anywhere. For in-person sessions in the Ashburn area, I typically meet students at a local library or the Barnes & Noble in One Loudoun for a safe and quiet environment.
